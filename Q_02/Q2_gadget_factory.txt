A gadget factory has been quite successful for the past 
10 years and Ms.Marry, the manager of the company wondering 
whether to expand the factory this year or not. The cost to
expand factory is $2M. With no expansion, expected revenue is $4M 
if the economy stays good; while only $1.5M if the economy is bad. 
If manager expands the factory, expected to receive $7M. if economy 
is good and $3M if economy is bad. Assume that there is a 45% chance 
of a good economy and a 55% chance of a bad economy. Draw a Decision 
Tree showing these choices.
